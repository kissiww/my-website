№1. Akame da Kill

Plot:

The story takes place in a fictional empire that is plagued by corruption, poverty, and brutality. Tatsumi, a young swordsman from a rural area, sets out for the capital city to earn money and save his village from poverty. Upon arriving in the city, he is confronted with a harsh reality: the capital is merely a facade of prosperity. Behind the façade lies a system ruled by a corrupt and violent prime minister. Tatsumi's journey takes him into the clutches of the revolutionary group known as the Night Raid. They specialize in eliminating corrupt officials and other dangerous elements that threaten the safety of citizens. The members of the group explain to Tatsumi that their goal is to overthrow the corrupt regime and build a new, just country. After a series of events, including learning the truth about his fallen comrades, Tatsumi joins the Night Raid. Another side is involved in the confrontation with the revolutionaries - the elite unit, the Jaegers, was created to destroy the Night Raid. Both sides use teigu, ancient artifacts that date back over a thousand years. These teigu are deadly weapons, armor, or power-ups that require a special compatibility with a person to control.
